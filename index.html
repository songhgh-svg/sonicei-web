Honest answers to the objections we hear most from procurement managers and project leads on E&I commissioning contracts.

Our workmanship commitment is written into the contract: any E&I defect attributable to our work is rectified at our cost, with no change order. We use rigorous manual QA/QC processes on every project, and we are currently integrating AI-Punch™ (beta) to catch issues earlier in construction — not at punch-out. If it's our mistake, we fix it.
